How to Play

Controls

W / SDrive Forward / Reverse A / DSteer Left / Right SpaceJump (double-tap to flip) ShiftBoost CToggle Ball Cam Q / EAir Roll Left / Right XHandbrake / Powerslide TabScoreboard

Tips

  • Collect glowing pads on the field to refill boost.
  • Large corner pads give full boost (100), small pads give 12.
  • Use ball cam (C) to track the ball, toggle off for dribbling.
  • Flip into the ball for powerful shots — jump then tilt and jump again.
  • Supersonic speed (gold trail) lets you demolish opponents on contact.
